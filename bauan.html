    Countdown Begins: University Students Gear Up for Nearing Midterm Examinations!

    Philippines, 2023 Oct 6

    • As the academic year progresses, the tension on Bauan University is palpable as students prepare to face their midterm examinations. Students are gearing up for what could be the most critical academic challenge of their year.The midterm examinations, often seen as the culmination of months of hard work and dedication, will put students' knowledge and skills to the test.
    • For many, these exams hold the key to their academic success and future careers. University professors and instructors are also playing a crucial role in helping students prepare. Many are holding extra review sessions, offering guidance on study strategies, and providing additional resources to aid in the revision process. The approaching final exams are not only a test of academic knowledge but also a test of resilience and time management skills.
    • Students are often juggling multiple subjects and assignments, which requires careful planning and organization to ensure they can cover all the necessary material. The Centro Escolar University is wishing their students the best of luck as they embark on this mid leg exam of the academic year, confident that their hard work and dedication will pay off in the end.

    EVENTS

    Halloween Thrills Await

    Halloween is just around the corner, promising spine-tingling fun. The school's Halloween committee has been hard at work planning a series of spooky events and surprises. The school gymnasium is being transformed into a haunted house, complete with ghouls, ghosts, and surprises lurking around every corner. Students can also look forward to a Halloween dance and a thrilling scavenger hunt.

    Spreading Holiday Joy

    The halls of University of Bauan have been echoing with the melodious voices of students and staff as they prepare to bring the magic of Christmas to life through music. From classic carols like "Silent Night" and "Deck the Halls" to contemporary favorites such as "All I Want for Christmas Is You," the repertoire for this year's Christmas carol performances is as diverse as it is enchanting.

    Christmas Cheer Takes Over

    The aroma of hot cocoa, the sound of jingling bells, and twinkling lights have begun to transform University of Bauan into a winter wonderland. Students and staff have joined hands to decorate the school, with classrooms adorned with colorful ornaments, wreaths, and handcrafted snowflakes.
